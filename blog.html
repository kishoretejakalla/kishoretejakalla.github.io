Sunday Getaway by Stardust Adventures

May 30, 2018

Travel with Stardust Adventures this Sunday to Yerravaram, a place rather unheard of, located just 100kms from Vizag where there is an amazing waterfall to which we could trek through the following stream. The rich greens around with tall trees, dense woods, coffee plantations, streams villages and so on would treat you to an amazing adventure.

Followed by which we have a sumptuous lunch at a local dhaba 20kms from there with some delicious food. And then move to the beautiful reservoir of Tajangi.

After all this we visit Kondakarla Ava, a bird sanctuary to cool down, where you could indulge in some boating in the lake there. And return to Vizag from there on.
